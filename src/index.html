How it works

An affiliate program is when a business rewards an affiliate for customer brought by him. The identification is usually done via a unique link that belongs to the affiliate that leads to the product.

When you buy something online from a store that has an affiliate program, a share of your payment is intended to be given to an affiliate, but they get to keep it when no one redirected you.

Never Buy Direct aims to claim back that share and give to creative people like content creators and open-source contributors. When you buy something through Never Buy Direct you'll be redirected to the product with an affiliate code that belongs to one of the subscribed creators, that way they receive their small share without costing you a thing.

Never Buy Direct is fully transparent, every partner has the same chances as any other, anyone can see the list of partners in the public JSON here.
